About Span Healthcare

Innovative healthcare solutions combining finance and technology

Who We Are

Span Healthcare LTD. is a leading health fin-tech company based in Abuja, Nigeria. We specialize in providing innovative healthcare financing solutions that combine savings, investments, and telemedicine services.

Our mission is to make healthcare accessible and affordable for everyone, especially in underserved markets. We believe that quality healthcare should be available to all, regardless of economic status.

Our Innovative Services

Health Financing
